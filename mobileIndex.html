Why A Developer?

As part of a school trip to New York City, I was once on the trading floor of a multinational bank. I was surprised by what I heard. The floor was quieter than most university libraries. The class was partnered with those working there. Most students were paired with traders, but I was fortunate to speak with a programmer. He said something to me that has stuck with me since. “My job is to put these traders out of work.” I was witnessing the effects of technology firsthand. As the world becomes more digitized day by day, I have developed a passion for technology, learning languages, such as JavaScript, Python, CSS, HTML, and SQL.
